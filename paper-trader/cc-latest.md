## The strategy (papertrader/strategies/swing.py)
A multi-signal SWING (holds days). The system runs once/day on CLOSE and fills
at the NEXT open (T→T+1), so every entry/exit is a next-open fill — never
intraday.

- **Universe:** `papertrader/sp500_universe.py` (~500-name best-effort S&P 500
  snapshot; refreshed by `tools/update_sp500_universe.py`). No S&P 500 list
  existed before — `update_universe.py` fetches the Nasdaq-100.
- **Entry (ALL FOUR on close T → buy open T+1):** breakout (close > prior 20d
  high) + trend (close > SMA50) + volume (today > 1.5× its 20d average; volume
  unavailable ⇒ condition NOT met) + RSI(14) in [50, 70]. Rank by breakout
  strength (% above the breakout level), top 5, equal-weight; the existing
  small_account_fill makes $100 buy the affordable subset.
- **Exit (ANY → sell open T+1; precedence stop > take-profit > move-over >
  time):** stop −8% · take-profit +20% (optional) · close < SMA10 · 20-day time
  stop. Hebrew reasons + realized P&L via the engine.
- **Two new portfolios** swing_100 / swing_10k → now **9 strategies × 2 sizes =
  18 portfolios.** Regime filter OFF by default (config to enable later).
