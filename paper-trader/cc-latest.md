### FIX 1 — partial-exit sells shown distinctly
A pending SELL with `partial:true` (e.g. `{side:"SELL", qty:5, partial:true, reason:
"מימוש חלקי +17% — נמכרה מחצית, סטופ הועלה לכניסה"}`) now renders on its own row:
`♻️ מימוש חלקי: מכירת {qty} יח׳ {ticker} · {reason}` in **green** (`.pend.partial`,
distinct from a full sell's muted grey and an unaffordable amber `.skip`). The qty
makes it clear only PART of the position is sold; the redundant leading "מימוש חלקי"
is stripped from the reason. A full sell (no `partial`) keeps the normal
`⏳ מכירה: T1 · T2` line. New shared `sellLinesHTML`/`partialSellRow` helpers are used
by **both** render paths (swing's structured path and the affordability path), so
partials look the same on every strategy. Sells are read from the RAW pending orders
(affordability() only kept tickers, dropping `partial`/`qty`/`reason`).

### FIX 2 — "held by N strategies" tag on the combined portfolio
For `combined_*` holdings, the per-holding sub-line now shows the `sources`:
- `sources.length > 1` → `מוחזק ע״י {N} אסטרטגיות (s1 · s2)` (a ticker deduped from
  several strategies),
- `sources.length === 1` → `מ-{strategy}`.
Only the combined portfolio uses this (other portfolios have no `sources`); strategy
names are Latin identifiers → `applyRtl` isolates each in `<bdi>`. For combined the
sub-line is just the sources (no rank/entry/current — combined is a snapshot blend).
